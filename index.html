Introduction

League of Legends is a strategy game that consists of two teams and each team have their own nexus(pic. 1, 1), inhibitor(pic. 1, 2), and three turrets(pic. 1, 3) for each lane.
The map of league of legends or summoners rift have three different lanes and a jungle on both sides, each with their own players who are playing the role that is assigned for that lane.
Roles such as a midlaner, adc, support, toplaner, and a jungler each have their important roles that will impact the state of the game as they go along in the future.
I will discuss each lanes and the different roles that is present in the game as well as the most common strategies that player who just started playing will need.

The Whole Map




(Lanes)

Mid Lane

The middle lane or mid lane is the shortest lane and is located at the center of the map. Mages and assassins are mostly used in this lane.


Bot Lane

The bottom lane or bot lane is a long lane that is located at the bottom side of the map. Ad carries and support go in partners in this lane.


Top Lane

The bottom lane or bot lane is a long lane that is located at the bottom side of the map. Ad carries and support go in partners in this lane.


Jungle

The jungle is where monster camps are located and is located between all the lanes at the map. Many kinds of junglers are often used but the most common and recommended ones are those who can stay healthy with heal and sustain.




(Roles)

Midlaner

The midlaner is the mage or assassin that is tasked to help teammates since they have fast access to any lanes. They also deal burst damages that is good for taking out low armor enemies.

Examples:

Annie

Zed

Leblanc

Kassadin





Attack Damage Carry (ADC)

The adc or ad carry is the ranged champion that is tasked to deal the big damages to the enemy but has low health and armor which makes them easy to kill when caught by surprise.

Examples:

Ashe

Caitlyn

Ezreal

Tristana





Support

The support is an important partner of the adc since they give assistance or protection for the adc to become stronger in the late game.

Examples:

Thresh

Soraka

Braum

Blitzcrank





Toplaner

The toplaner is the bruiser or tank that either deals damage per second or tanks for the whole team. They have easy access to farming for gold and experience thus making them stronger in the late game.

Examples:

Sett

Jax

Ornn

Gankplank





Jungle

The jungler is the one who gives early games assistance to lanes by giving them a gank. They also get gold and experience from monster camps since minions are not available in this area.

Examples:

Lee Sin

Elise

Master Yi

Udyr





Basic Actions in Game

Farming

Farming is an act of killing the minions when they are at very low health so you can grab their gold in which will be used to buy items to be used for advantages later on.


Ganking

Ganking is an act of helping a laner by going in the lane by surprise and cathing the enemy off guard for a kill.
